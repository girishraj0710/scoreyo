## 🔧 QUALITY STANDARDS (Cambridge-Based)

### All Questions Must Have:
✅ **4 options** (A, B, C, D)  
✅ **1 clear correct answer** (0-3 index)  
✅ **Rich explanation** (80+ chars, includes pattern/rule)  
✅ **40/40/20 distribution** (easy/medium/hard)  
✅ **Appropriate level** (beginner/intermediate/advanced)  
✅ **Proper SQL escaping** (double single quotes for apostrophes)

### Reading Comprehension Standards:
- **Passage length:** 100-150 words (not 200+)
- **Authentic content:** Real topics, not templates
- **Varied question types:**
  - Main idea
  - Detail questions
  - Inference
  - Vocabulary in context
  - Author's purpose
- **Cambridge IELTS format:** Similar to Academic Reading

### Explanation Quality:
- **Minimum 80 characters**
- **Include pattern/rule:** "This pattern...", "Use this rule...", "The form is..."
- **Explain why wrong answers are wrong** (for medium/hard)
- **Provide learning value:** Not just "The answer is X"
