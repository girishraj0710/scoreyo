## Error Categories Covered

### 1. Word Order (Hindi SOV → English SVO)
- Verb placement
- Adjective order
- Question formation
- Adverb positioning

### 2. Articles (Hindi has none → English requires specific usage)
- Indefinite a/an
- Definite the
- Zero article
- Fixed phrases

### 3. Prepositions (Hindi postpositions → English prepositions)
- Time: at/on/in
- Place: at/on/in
- Direction: to/into
- Fixed verb + preposition collocations

### 4. Tenses (Hindi aspect differences)
- Present simple vs progressive
- Past simple vs perfect
- Stative verbs (cannot be progressive)
- Duration expressions

### 5. Literal Translations (Hindi idioms → English equivalents)
- "I have doubt" → "I have a question"
- "What is your good name?" → "What is your name?"
- "Do the needful" → "Complete the task"
- Verb collocations (do/make/take/have)
