## 🔧 IMPLEMENTATION APPROACH

### Step-by-Step Generation (Zero Errors):

1. **Generate ONE topic at a time** (not bulk workflows)
2. **Test SQL syntax immediately** (catch errors early)
3. **Insert and verify** (confirm counts, check formatting)
4. **Iterate** (only move to next after success)

### Quality Checklist:
- ✅ All instruction words have colons (Choose:, Error:, etc.)
- ✅ Apostrophes escaped as '' (double quotes)
- ✅ Options as JSON array TEXT: '["opt1", "opt2", "opt3", "opt4"]'
- ✅ Correct_answer as 0-based index (0, 1, 2, 3)
- ✅ Difficulty distribution: 40% easy, 40% medium, 20% hard
- ✅ Beginner-friendly explanations with patterns
