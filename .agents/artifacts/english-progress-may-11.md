### Question Format Standards
1. **Multiple Choice:** Most common (80% of questions)

   - Distractors should be plausible
   - Test common mistakes

2. **Explanations are Critical:**
   - Must teach the concept, not just state answer
   - 2-3 sentences minimum
   - Include grammar rules or vocabulary context
