# Krakkify Quality-First Strategy

**Philosophy Change:** Quality > Quantity
**Goal:** 20,000 HIGH-QUALITY questions > 90,000 mediocre questions

## Why This Approach?

**Old Thinking:**
- ❌ Generate 90K AI questions (75% accuracy = 22K bad questions!)
- ❌ Students lose trust when they hit wrong answers
- ❌ You can't verify every topic yourself

**New Thinking:**
- ✅ Start with 15-20K VERIFIED questions (95%+ accuracy)
- ✅ Students trust Krakkify as THE quality platform
- ✅ Grow gradually with only verified sources
- ✅ Every question has a source citation

## Phase 1: Verified Sources Only (Target: 20K Questions)

### Source 1: Official Previous Year Questions (PYQs)
**Where to get:**
- NTA official website (JEE, NEET)
- UPSC official portal
- State exam board websites
- Banking exam archives

**Quality:** 100% (exam setters create these)
**Expected:** 5,000 questions

### Source 2: NCERT Solutions

### Source 4: Academic Partnerships

## Quality Guarantees

### Every Question Must Have:
1. ✅ **Source citation** ("JEE Main 2024", "NCERT Class 12 Physics Ch 3")
2. ✅ **Verification status** ("Official PYQ", "Textbook", "Expert-Verified")
3. ✅ **Review date** (when last checked)
4. ✅ **Report mechanism** (users can flag issues)
