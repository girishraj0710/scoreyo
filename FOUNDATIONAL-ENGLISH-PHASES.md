# Foundational English - Complete Phase Structure

**Learning Path:** Foundation Builder (A1 → B1)  
**Total Topics:** 40+ topics  
**Lesson Style:** Premium, pattern-based, mobile-first micro-lessons

---

## 📚 PHASE 1: ABSOLUTE BASICS (Week 1-2) - A1 Level
**Goal:** From zero to speaking simple sentences

### Module 1.1: Building Blocks (3 topics)
1. ✅ **"I Am, You Are, They Are"** (am/is/are basics)
   - Topic ID: `be-verb-present`
   - Pattern: [Subject] + am/is/are + [Adjective/Noun]

2. ✅ **"This Is My..."** (Pointing to things)
   - Topic ID: `demonstratives-basic`
   - Pattern: This/That + is + [Noun]

3. ✅ **"I Have, You Have"** (Possession basics)
   - Topic ID: `have-got-basic`
   - Pattern: [Subject] + have/has + [Object]

### Module 1.2: Articles & Basic Words (2 topics)
4. ✅ **"Using A, An, The"** (Articles)
   - Topic ID: `articles`
   - Pattern: a/an before general things, the before specific

5. ✅ **"Describing Things"** (Basic adjectives)
   - Topic ID: `adjectives-basic`
   - Pattern: [Adjective] + [Noun] / It is + [Adjective]

---

## 📚 PHASE 2: DAILY ACTIONS (Week 3-4) - A1 Level
**Goal:** Talk about daily routines and habits

### Module 2.1: Present Simple (4 topics)
6. ✅ **"What I Do Every Day"** (Present Simple - I/You/We/They)
   - Topic ID: `present-simple`
   - Pattern: I/You/We/They + [Action Word]

7. ✅ **"What He/She Does"** (Present Simple - He/She/It)
   - Topic ID: `present-simple-third-person`
   - Pattern: He/She/It + [Action Word + s/es]

8. ✅ **"I Don't, He Doesn't"** (Negative form)
   - Topic ID: `present-simple-negative`
   - Pattern: I/We/You/They + don't + [Action] / He/She/It + doesn't + [Action]

9. ✅ **"Do You...? Does She...?"** (Questions)
   - Topic ID: `present-simple-questions`
   - Pattern: Do + I/you/we/they + [Action]? / Does + he/she/it + [Action]?

### Module 2.2: Present Continuous (3 topics)
10. ✅ **"What I'm Doing Right Now"** (Present Continuous)
    - Topic ID: `present-continuous`
    - Pattern: I am/He is/They are + [Action + ing]

11. ✅ **"Not Doing Something Now"** (Negative continuous)
    - Topic ID: `present-continuous-negative`
    - Pattern: I'm not/He isn't/They aren't + [Action + ing]

12. ✅ **"What Are You Doing?"** (Continuous questions)
    - Topic ID: `present-continuous-questions`
    - Pattern: Am I/Is he/Are they + [Action + ing]?

---

## 📚 PHASE 3: TALKING ABOUT THE PAST (Week 5-6) - A1-A2 Level
**Goal:** Tell stories about what happened

### Module 3.1: Past Simple (4 topics)
13. ✅ **"What I Did Yesterday"** (Past Simple regular verbs)
    - Topic ID: `past-simple`
    - Pattern: I/You/He/She/We/They + [Action + ed]

14. ✅ **"Common Irregular Verbs"** (go-went, eat-ate, etc.)
    - Topic ID: `past-simple-irregular`
    - Pattern: Subject + [Irregular Past Form]

15. ✅ **"I Didn't Do That"** (Past negative)
    - Topic ID: `past-simple-negative`
    - Pattern: Subject + didn't + [Base Action]

16. ✅ **"Did You...?"** (Past questions)
    - Topic ID: `past-simple-questions`
    - Pattern: Did + Subject + [Base Action]?

### Module 3.2: Past Continuous (2 topics)
17. ✅ **"What I Was Doing"** (Past continuous)
    - Topic ID: `past-continuous`
    - Pattern: I was/They were + [Action + ing]

18. ✅ **"While I Was..."** (Two actions at same time)
    - Topic ID: `past-continuous-while-when`
    - Pattern: I was [doing X] when [Y happened]

---

## 📚 PHASE 4: TALKING ABOUT THE FUTURE (Week 7-8) - A2 Level
**Goal:** Make plans and predictions

### Module 4.1: Future Forms (3 topics)
19. ✅ **"I Will..."** (Simple future predictions)
    - Topic ID: `future-simple`
    - Pattern: I/You/He/She/We/They + will + [Action]

20. ✅ **"I'm Going To..."** (Future plans)
    - Topic ID: `going-to-future`
    - Pattern: I am/He is/They are + going to + [Action]

21. ✅ **"Will vs Going To"** (Choosing the right form)
    - Topic ID: `future-comparison`
    - Pattern: will (predictions) vs going to (plans)

---

## 📚 PHASE 5: CONNECTING IDEAS (Week 9-10) - A2 Level
**Goal:** Make longer, more natural sentences

### Module 5.1: Present Perfect (3 topics)
22. ✅ **"I Have Done This Before"** (Present Perfect)
    - Topic ID: `present-perfect`
    - Pattern: I have/He has + [Past Participle]

23. ✅ **"Ever, Never, Already, Yet"** (Time markers)
    - Topic ID: `present-perfect-time-markers`
    - Pattern: I have + [never/already/just] + [Past Participle]

24. ✅ **"For vs Since"** (Duration markers)
    - Topic ID: `present-perfect-for-since`
    - Pattern: I have lived here + [for 5 years / since 2020]

### Module 5.2: Conjunctions & Connectors (2 topics)
25. ✅ **"And, But, Or, Because"** (Basic connectors)
    - Topic ID: `conjunctions-basic`
    - Pattern: Sentence 1 + [connector] + Sentence 2

26. ✅ **"When, Before, After, While"** (Time connectors)
    - Topic ID: `time-connectors`
    - Pattern: I [action 1] + [connector] + I [action 2]

---

## 📚 PHASE 6: ADVANCED STRUCTURES (Week 11-12) - A2-B1 Level
**Goal:** Sound more fluent and natural

### Module 6.1: Modal Verbs (3 topics)
27. ✅ **"Can, Could"** (Ability and permission)
    - Topic ID: `modals-can-could`
    - Pattern: I can/could + [Action]

28. ✅ **"Should, Must"** (Advice and obligation)
    - Topic ID: `modals-should-must`
    - Pattern: You should/must + [Action]

29. ✅ **"May, Might, Will"** (Possibility and future)
    - Topic ID: `modals-possibility`
    - Pattern: It may/might/will + [Happen]

### Module 6.2: Passive Voice (2 topics)
30. ✅ **"It Is Made, It Was Built"** (Passive basics)
    - Topic ID: `passive-voice-basic`
    - Pattern: [Object] + is/was + [Past Participle]

31. ✅ **"Passive in Different Tenses"** (All tenses)
    - Topic ID: `passive-voice-tenses`
    - Pattern: Passive across present/past/future

### Module 6.3: Reported Speech (2 topics)
32. ✅ **"He Said That..."** (Reporting statements)
    - Topic ID: `reported-speech-statements`
    - Pattern: He said + [that] + [shifted sentence]

33. ✅ **"He Asked If..."** (Reporting questions)
    - Topic ID: `reported-speech-questions`
    - Pattern: He asked + [if/whether] + [shifted question]

---

## 📚 PHASE 7: SPECIAL TOPICS (Week 13-14) - A2-B1 Level
**Goal:** Handle specific situations

### Module 7.1: Conditionals (3 topics)
34. ✅ **"If I Do, I Will"** (First conditional)
    - Topic ID: `conditionals-first`
    - Pattern: If + [present], [will + action]

35. ✅ **"If I Did, I Would"** (Second conditional)
    - Topic ID: `conditionals-second`
    - Pattern: If + [past], [would + action]

36. ✅ **"If I Had Done, I Would Have"** (Third conditional)
    - Topic ID: `conditionals-third`
    - Pattern: If + [past perfect], [would have + past participle]

### Module 7.2: Relative Clauses (2 topics)
37. ✅ **"Who, Which, That"** (Defining clauses)
    - Topic ID: `relative-clauses-defining`
    - Pattern: The person who/The thing which/that...

38. ✅ **"Where, When, Whose"** (More relatives)
    - Topic ID: `relative-clauses-advanced`
    - Pattern: The place where/The time when/The person whose...

---

## 📚 PHASE 8: POLISH & PRACTICE (Week 15-16) - B1 Level
**Goal:** Fix common mistakes and sound natural

### Module 8.1: Common Errors (3 topics)
39. ✅ **"Common Mistakes Indians Make"** (L1 interference)
    - Topic ID: `common-mistakes-indian-learners`
    - Pattern: Wrong vs Right + Explanation

40. ✅ **"Tricky Grammar Points"** (Subject-verb agreement, etc.)
    - Topic ID: `tricky-grammar`
    - Pattern: Special cases and exceptions

41. ✅ **"Phrasal Verbs Basics"** (Get up, look for, etc.)
    - Topic ID: `phrasal-verbs-basic`
    - Pattern: Verb + Preposition = New Meaning
